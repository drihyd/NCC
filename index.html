A LEGACY OF TRANSFORMATION

Over 4 decades of infra-excellence

It was in 1978 that the seeds of helping India transform to an economic power was sown by Dr. AVS Raju, Padma Shri Awardee and India’s poineering infra-visionary when he floated Nagarjuna Construction Company Ltd. as a partnership firm. From very modest beginnings as a construction company about four decades ago, today NCC Limited stands tall as a conglomerate encompassing various facets of construction and infrastructure building.

Committed to a brighter world...

Deligence can accomplish wonders!

For over four decades, we never sidetracked from this belief. and have leapfrogged from being just a construction industry player to a Billion dollar multifunctional infrastructure conglomerate.
