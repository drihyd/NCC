From very modest beginnings as a construction company about four decades ago, today NCC Limited stands tall as a conglomerate encompassing various facets of construction and infrastructure building.

OUR PRESENCE

Regional Offices across India

NCC Limited has made a mark in the country as the second largest listed construction company in terms of revenue,with well diversified activities comprising building & housing, roads, water & environment, irrigation, electrical, metals, mining and railways. The group has expanded its footprints to have a pan India presence with offices across 13 cities in Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Telagana, Karnataka, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal and Tamil Nadu.
